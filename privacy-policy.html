Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this e-commerce website handles basic information and browser storage when users browse products, use the shopping cart, or change site preferences.

Information We Use

This demo website does not require users to create an account. It may store basic shopping preferences in the browser, including cart items, selected currency, and cookie consent choice.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential browser storage to keep the website functional. This includes remembering cart contents, currency selection, and whether the user accepted or rejected non-essential cookies.

How We Use Information

  • To display products and shopping cart information correctly.
  • To remember user preferences such as currency selection.
  • To record the cookie banner choice so it is not repeatedly shown.

Third-Party Services

The website may load external resources such as product images, icons, fonts, currency rates, or flag images. These third-party providers may process requests according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. This coursework demo does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

User Choices

Users can clear browser storage at any time through their browser settings. Clearing storage will reset cart items, currency preferences, and cookie consent status.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the project team through the repository or the contact information provided on the website.
